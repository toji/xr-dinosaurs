XR Dinosaurs

Welcome to the web's virtual Dinosaur Park!

We've used the magic of your browser to bring back a friendly pack of prehistoric pals.

Our dinosaurs can be viewed with a variety of Virtual Reality headsets, Augmented Reality headsets and phones, or directly in your browser.

  • See the Dinosaurs in your Browser.
  • Visit our Dinosaurs in Virtual Reality.
  • Bring a Dinosaur home with Augmented Reality.

Rawr!

  • Anklyosaurus

    She may be covered in spikes, but our Ankylosaur is actually quite cuddly and loves to meet new friends! Think of her as a big, prehistoric puppy.

  • Brachiosaurus

    Our biggest dinosaur by far, she weighs as much as 12 elephants! But don't worry, she's very friendly and we've taught her to be extremely careful about where she's stepping!

  • Pachycephalosaurus

    This sweet "Pachy" is one of our older dinosaurs. She's a bit shy and gets nervous around new people, but is incredibly gentle.

  • Parasaurolophus

    Our Parasaurolophus is a mischevious, silly dinosaur. The long crest on her head acts like a trumpet, which she loves using to surprise park guests.

  • Utahraptor

    You've probably heard these smart dinosaurs called "Velociraptors", but those were actually quite small. Our Utahraptor is much closer in size to the dinosaurs you know and love from the big screen.

  • Stegosaurus

    Strong and proud, the Stegosaurus is one of our more popular dinosaurs. Her favorite thing is getting her plates scrubbed clean so they stay such a beautiful, shiny red.

  • Triceratops

    Our youngest dinosaur, this juvenile Triceratops is a smaller than a full grown adult would be. Spunky and playful, she loves being the center of attention.

  • Tyrannosaurus Rex

    Don't let the big teeth scare you, our T-Rex is really a big softie that loves showing off. Her hobbies include making stompy noises with her feet and going for runs around her pen.
